Ludex Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This privacy policy document outlines the types of personal information that is received and collected by Ludex and how it is used.

Information Collection and Use

Ludex collects personal information such as email addresses and Google account information for user authentication and account management purposes.

Data Storage and Security

All user data is stored securely using Firebase services. Data in transit is encrypted to ensure privacy and security.

Third-Party Services

We use third-party services like Firebase for authentication, database, and analytics. These services comply with industry standards for data protection and privacy.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at ing.cvalera@gmail.com.

Account Deletion Request

If you wish to delete your account and all associated data, please follow the steps below:

Steps to Request Account Deletion

  • Step 1: Send an email to ing.cvalera@gmail.com with the subject "Account Deletion Request".
  • Step 2: Include your username and the email address associated with your Ludex account in the email.
  • Step 3: You will receive a confirmation email. Follow the instructions in the email to complete the account deletion process.

Data Retention Policy

Once your account is deleted, all associated data will be permanently removed from our servers. This includes all your game lists, preferences, and account information. Please note that this action is irreversible.

Additional Information

If you have any questions regarding the deletion of your account or the data retention policy, please contact us at ing.cvalera@gmail.com.
